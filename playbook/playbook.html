Patient Acquisition · Paid Search

Google Ads

Capture high-intent patients at the exact moment they search for a clinic or treatment.

What it is

A managed pay-per-click advertising program on Google's network. We create, run, and continuously optimize campaigns so the clinic appears prominently when prospective patients search for relevant treatments and specialties.

Problem it solves

Most clinics have no systematic way to reach patients who are ready to book. Word-of-mouth is unpredictable and slow. Google Ads puts the clinic in front of high-intent audiences on demand, generating a consistent and scalable flow of appointment requests.

What it includes
  • Search Ads — Text ads that appear when patients search for treatments, specialties, or clinic names ("physiotherapist near me", "dental implants [city]"). Highest intent format.
  • Performance Max — AI-driven campaigns that automatically distribute ads across Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail, and Maps from a single setup. Optimises for bookings and form submissions.
  • Display / Remarketing — Visual banner ads shown to people who visited the clinic's website or landing page but didn't convert — bringing them back to book.
  • YouTube Ads — Short video ads before relevant health content, ideal for building trust and showcasing clinic facilities or patient testimonials.
  • Keyword research & negative keywords — Monthly updates to exclude irrelevant searches and lower wasted spend.
  • Ad copy A/B testing — Continuous testing of headlines and descriptions to improve click-through rate.
  • Conversion tracking setup — Every call, form fill, and booking is tracked back to the ad that generated it.

How it works

1
Onboarding audit. We audit existing accounts (if any), analyse competitor spend, and identify the highest-value search terms for the clinic's specialty and location.
2
Campaign architecture. We structure campaigns by service line (e.g., orthopaedics, aesthetics, dental) and match types, ensuring budget is allocated where intent is highest.
3
Ad creation. We write compliant, compelling ad copy with clear calls-to-action ("Book your consultation today") and link each ad to a dedicated landing page.
4
Launch & monitoring. Campaigns go live and we monitor performance daily in the first two weeks, adjusting bids, budgets, and targeting based on real data.
5
Monthly optimisation. We review search terms, pause underperforming ads, test new copy, and expand on what works — improving cost per lead every cycle.

Patient Acquisition · Social Ads

Meta Ads

Reach and educate prospective patients on Instagram and Facebook before they even start searching.

What it is

Targeted advertising campaigns on Instagram and Facebook designed to generate awareness, leads, and appointment bookings — using demographic, behavioural, and interest-based targeting specific to the clinic's ideal patient profile.

Problem it solves

Many patients don't search Google until they already feel the problem acutely. Meta Ads create demand earlier in the journey — building trust and capturing interest from people who match the ideal patient profile before they're actively looking elsewhere.

What it includes
  • Lead Generation campaigns — Native lead forms inside Instagram/Facebook that pre-fill patient contact info for frictionless opt-ins.
  • Traffic campaigns — Drive targeted users to the landing page or booking calendar.
  • Retargeting campaigns — Re-engage website visitors, video viewers, and form openers who didn't convert.
  • Lookalike audiences — We build audiences that mirror the clinic's best existing patients for efficient scaling.
  • Creative production direction — Ad copy, hooks, and creative briefs for static images, carousels, and short video formats.
  • Pixel & Conversion API setup — Accurate tracking of every lead and booking back to the specific ad and audience.

How it works

1
Audience definition. We map the clinic's ideal patient by age, location, health interests, and behaviours. We build cold, warm, and hot audiences for each funnel stage.
2
Creative strategy. We define the messaging angle for each audience (awareness vs. urgency vs. social proof) and brief the creative accordingly.
3
Campaign launch. Campaigns are structured with clear budget splits between prospecting and retargeting, with A/B creative tests from day one.
4
Lead handoff. All leads captured via Meta flow instantly into the Navigy CRM, triggering an immediate WhatsApp follow-up sequence.
5
Weekly optimisation. We cut underperforming creatives, scale winners, refresh audiences, and update messaging based on engagement data.

Patient Acquisition · Organic

Local SEO

Get found on Google Maps and local search results — for free, every single day.

What it is

A set of ongoing optimisation actions that improve the clinic's visibility in local organic search results — so patients searching "physiotherapist near me" or "[specialty] [city]" find the clinic without any ad spend.

Problem it solves

Most clinics are invisible in local search beyond the first few results. Patients rarely scroll past the top three. Without active local SEO, clinics pay for every visitor forever. Local SEO builds compounding, free visibility that grows over time.

What it includes
  • Google Business Profile optimisation — Full setup and ongoing management (categories, services, photos, hours, Q&A, posts).
  • Local keyword integration — City, neighbourhood, and treatment keywords embedded naturally across web pages.
  • On-page technical fixes — Title tags, meta descriptions, headings, and internal links optimised for local relevance.
  • Page speed improvements — Core Web Vitals tuning, especially for mobile — critical for local pack rankings.
  • Citation consistency — NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency audit across directories.
  • Review acquisition support — Automated WhatsApp/SMS requests to patients post-appointment to drive Google review volume.
  • Monthly GBP posts — Fresh content on the Business Profile to keep the listing active and ranked.

How it works

1
Audit. We audit the current GBP listing, website on-page SEO, local keyword rankings, and competitor positions.
2
GBP full optimisation. We complete and structure the profile to maximise Local Pack eligibility — the three listings that appear in map results above organic results.
3
On-site implementation. Local keywords, schema markup, and technical improvements are applied to the website.
4
Review generation engine. Automated post-appointment messages via WhatsApp prompt happy patients to leave a Google review, growing the review count sustainably.
5
Monthly maintenance. New GBP posts, review responses, keyword monitoring, and incremental page improvements keep rankings climbing.
💡

Local SEO and Google Ads work best together. Ads deliver immediate leads while SEO builds free organic traffic over 3–6 months. The combination reduces the clinic's long-term cost per patient significantly.

Patient Acquisition · Local Presence

Google Business Profile

Turn the clinic's Google listing into a patient-converting asset that works 24 hours a day.

What it is

Full management and optimisation of the clinic's Google Business Profile — the listing that appears on Google Search and Maps when patients look for a clinic locally. A well-managed GBP is often the first impression a patient has of the clinic.

Problem it solves

Most clinics set up their GBP profile once and never touch it again. Incomplete, outdated, or low-review profiles lose patients to competitors with better-managed listings — even when the clinic is technically superior.

What it includes
  • Full profile setup and verification — Business name, categories, description, services, attributes, contact details, website link.
  • Photo management — Upload and regularly refresh photos of the clinic, team, treatments, and before/after results (where compliant).
  • Monthly GBP posts — Health tips, promotions, new services, and seasonal content keep the profile active and visible.
  • Automated review requests — Post-appointment WhatsApp messages prompt patients to leave reviews on Google. Retry logic ensures higher response rates.
  • Review response management — All reviews (positive and negative) are responded to professionally and promptly via our software's automated response system.
  • Q&A management — Common patient questions are pre-populated and answered in the Q&A section to improve conversion from the listing.
  • Messaging & call activation — Direct messaging and call buttons configured so patients can contact the clinic in one tap.

How it works

1
Audit & claim. We audit the existing profile (or create a new one), claim ownership, and resolve any duplicate listings.
2
Complete optimisation. Every available field is completed with clinic-specific, keyword-rich content. We prioritise the sections that most influence Local Pack rankings.
3
Review engine activation. We connect the review request automation so every discharged or post-visit patient receives a polite, timed WhatsApp prompt to leave a review.
4
Ongoing posting. Monthly content is published to the profile — keeping it fresh and signalling activity to Google's ranking algorithm.

Conversion Systems · Lead Capture

Landing Pages

Dedicated pages with one job: turn ad traffic into booked consultations.

What it is

Purpose-built, standalone web pages created for specific campaigns or treatment services. Unlike a general website, a landing page has no navigation distractions — every element is engineered to guide the visitor toward a single action: booking or requesting a consultation.

Problem it solves

Sending ad traffic to a clinic's generic homepage wastes budget. The homepage has too much information and no clear next step. Landing pages isolate the offer, build trust fast, and make it frictionless to book — dramatically improving conversion rates from paid campaigns.

What it includes
  • Conversion-first design — Clean layout focused on the single action: book, call, or fill in the form. No unnecessary navigation or competing CTAs.
  • Clinic-specific messaging — Headlines, benefits, and social proof tailored to the treatment or specialty being promoted.
  • Trust elements — Doctor credentials, certifications, patient testimonials, before/after photos (compliant), and review badges.
  • Integrated booking calendar — Patients can select a consultation slot directly on the page — zero phone calls needed.
  • Lead form connected to CRM — Every submission creates a contact in the pipeline and triggers an instant WhatsApp welcome message.
  • Mobile-first, fast-loading — Optimised for Core Web Vitals; loads in under 2 seconds on mobile to prevent drop-off.
  • Google Ads Quality Score alignment — Content matches ad keywords to lower cost-per-click and improve ad rankings.

How it works

1
Brief & structure. We define the campaign goal, the target patient type, and the unique selling proposition for each landing page.
2
Copy & design. We write the persuasive copy and design the layout using proven conversion frameworks (problem → solution → proof → CTA).
3
CRM & automation connection. The form is wired to the pipeline. Submission → contact created → WhatsApp message sent → team notified. All within seconds.
4
Tracking setup. Google Analytics, Google Ads conversion pixels, and Meta Pixel are installed so every lead is attributed to its source.
5
Ongoing optimisation. We analyse heatmaps, form completion rates, and conversion data monthly, iterating copy or layout to improve results.

Conversion Systems · Website

Website Optimization

Make the clinic's existing website convert more visitors without rebuilding it from scratch.

What it is

A focused set of technical and copywriting improvements applied to the clinic's existing website to increase the percentage of visitors who take action — call, book, or fill in a contact form — without a full website rebuild.

Problem it solves

Many clinic websites attract organic traffic but convert very few visitors into patients. Poor mobile experience, slow load times, unclear calls-to-action, and lack of trust signals all cause visitors to leave without engaging. Optimisation fixes these leaks.

What it includes
  • CTA audit & implementation — Review and improve all calls-to-action site-wide. Ensure every page has a clear next step ("Book a consultation", "Call us now").
  • Mobile UX improvements — Fix layout issues, button sizing, font readability, and navigation on mobile devices.
  • Page speed optimisation — Compress images, leverage caching, remove render-blocking scripts, improve Core Web Vitals scores.
  • Trust signal placement — Add review badges, certifications, team bios, and media mentions to key pages.
  • Local keyword integration — City-specific keywords woven naturally into service pages and meta data.
  • Contact friction removal — Simplify forms, add WhatsApp chat widget, and embed a booking calendar on key pages.
  • Analytics setup — Install GA4, Google Search Console, and conversion events to make the site measurable.

How it works

1
Audit. We analyse the site's current conversion rate, page speed, mobile experience, and on-page SEO gaps using diagnostic tools.
2
Priority fix list. We rank issues by impact and implement the highest-value improvements first — focusing on pages that receive the most traffic.
3
Implementation. Our team applies the changes directly, coordinating with the clinic's web admin if needed.
4
Measurement. We set benchmark conversion rates before and track improvement monthly.
⚠️

Important: Website optimisation is not a full redesign. It targets the highest-impact changes. For clinics needing a complete site rebuild, that is scoped separately.

Lead Management · CRM

CRM Pipeline

A visual, real-time system that shows every lead's exact status — so the team always knows what to do next.

What it is

A fully configured patient pipeline inside the Navigy CRM. Every lead — from any source — enters automatically, moves through defined stages, and receives automated follow-up at each step. The team gets a single, clear view of all sales activity.

Problem it solves

Without a CRM, leads live in WhatsApp chats, emails, and spreadsheets. Leads get forgotten. No one knows who spoke to whom or what stage each patient is at. Appointments don't get followed up. The CRM eliminates that chaos with a structured, automated system.

  • Stage-based automation — Each pipeline stage triggers automatic actions: WhatsApp messages, email follow-ups, and team task assignments.
  • Lead scoring — Leads are tagged by source, service interest, and engagement level to help the team prioritise the most likely to convert.
  • Full contact history — Every call, message, and note is stored in the contact record — the team always has full context before engaging.

Automation · Messaging

WhatsApp Automation

Turn WhatsApp into a 24/7 patient communication engine — responsive, personalised, and automatic.

What it is

A suite of automated WhatsApp flows built inside the Navigy platform. From instant lead responses to appointment reminders and review requests — every touchpoint is automated, personalised, and connected to the CRM pipeline in real time.

Problem it solves

Clinics lose patients by responding too slowly. Studies show conversion rates drop sharply after 5 minutes of no response. Staff can't be available 24/7. WhatsApp automation ensures every enquiry gets an instant, professional reply — at any hour, any day.

What it includes
  • Instant lead response sequence — When a form is submitted or a message received, an automated WhatsApp reply is sent immediately, confirming receipt and setting expectations.
  • AI chatbot — 24/7 chatbot answers common questions (location, services, prices, availability) and guides patients toward booking without human intervention.
  • Appointment reminder sequence — Timed reminders sent 48h, 24h, and 2h before the appointment. Reduces no-shows significantly.
  • Review request automation — Triggered after a confirmed appointment: a friendly, timed WhatsApp asks satisfied patients to leave a Google review. Retry logic for non-responders.
  • WhatsApp widget on website — A chat button captures visitor details and initiates an automated conversation, converting passive browsers into leads.
  • Missed call follow-up — If a patient calls and no one answers, an automatic WhatsApp is sent within seconds: "Sorry we missed you — how can we help?"
  • Centralised inbox — All incoming WhatsApp conversations, alongside email, SMS, and social DMs, managed from a single platform.

How it works

1
Trigger. An action occurs — form submission, incoming WhatsApp, missed call, appointment scheduled, appointment completed.
2
Automation fires. The relevant sequence launches automatically. No human needed to initiate it.
3
Patient receives message. Personalised with their name and relevant details. Reads naturally — not robotic.
4
Response routed. If the patient replies, the message appears in the unified inbox and the team takes over the conversation where the bot left off.
5
CRM updated. All interactions are logged automatically in the patient's contact record.
💡

Human touch note: Automation handles the speed and scale. For appointment confirmations, a personal call from a team member remains the gold standard for building patient loyalty — automation supplements, it doesn't replace, human contact.

Automation · Email

Email Automation

Nurture leads and retain patients through targeted, automated email sequences that run in the background.

What it is

Automated email sequences that trigger based on patient or lead behaviour. From the first enquiry through onboarding, post-treatment follow-up, and retention — email automation maintains professional communication at every stage without manual effort.

Problem it solves

Clinics rarely have the capacity to send personalised follow-up emails to every lead and patient. Opportunities are missed at every stage of the journey — leads go cold, patients don't return, and upsell opportunities are never presented. Automation solves this at scale.

What it includes
  • Lead welcome sequence — Triggered when a new lead submits a form. 2–3 emails over 5 days building trust, presenting social proof, and pushing toward booking.
  • Appointment confirmation email — Detailed confirmation with date, time, location, preparation instructions, and a calendar invite.
  • Pre-appointment reminders — Sent in combination with WhatsApp reminders for maximum attendance rates.
  • Post-visit follow-up — Check-in email after treatment. Prompts for feedback, review requests, and next-step treatment suggestions.
  • Re-engagement campaigns — Automated sequences for patients who haven't returned in 3–6 months, offering a reason to come back.
  • Monthly health newsletters — Optional educational content to maintain top-of-mind awareness with the patient database.

How it works

1
Trigger event. A defined action (new lead, booking confirmed, appointment completed, 90 days of inactivity) activates the relevant email sequence.
2
Sequence runs. Emails send on a preset schedule. Each email is personalised with the patient's name and relevant details.
3
Behaviour-based branching. If a patient clicks a booking link, the "push to book" emails stop — the sequence adapts to avoid over-messaging.
4
Reporting. Open rates, click rates, and conversion events tracked monthly so sequences are continually improved.

Automation · Reactivation

Lead Reactivation

Revive old leads and lapsed patients who already know the clinic — the cheapest source of new appointments.

What it is

Automated multi-channel campaigns targeting people who previously enquired but never booked, or existing patients who haven't returned in 3+ months. These contacts already know the clinic — reactivating them costs far less than acquiring new leads.

Problem it solves

Most clinics have a database full of untapped leads and lapsed patients. These are warm contacts who showed intent at some point but fell through the cracks. Without a reactivation system, that value sits dormant. Reactivation campaigns unlock it.

What it includes
  • Cold lead reactivation — WhatsApp + email sequences targeting leads who enquired but never booked, sent after a defined period of inactivity (e.g., 30, 60, or 90 days).
  • Lapsed patient win-back — Automated outreach to patients who haven't returned in 3–6 months, with a personalised reason to book again.
  • Offer-based triggers — Reactivation messages include a specific incentive (limited-time consultation discount, seasonal health check offer) to increase response rates.
  • Multi-step sequences — 2–3 message touchpoints with escalating urgency to maximise conversions before the contact is archived.
  • CRM segmentation — Contacts are filtered by time since last contact, treatment type, and enquiry source for relevant personalisation.

How it works

1
Segment the database. The CRM filters all leads who haven't progressed past a set stage, and all patients inactive beyond a set date.
2
Sequence launches. A WhatsApp message is sent first (highest open rate). If no response after 48h, an email is sent. A final message follows after another 3 days.
3
Response handling. Contacts who reply are moved back into the active pipeline and the team follows up. Non-responders are archived after the sequence completes.
4
Continuous cycle. The reactivation segment is refreshed monthly — any new stale leads automatically enter the next scheduled campaign.

Content · Reputation

Review Campaigns

Systematically grow the clinic's Google reviews to build trust, improve rankings, and win patients from competitors.

What it is

An automated, ongoing system that requests Google reviews from patients at the optimal moment post-appointment — via WhatsApp. The system handles timing, retry logic, and volume to build a consistent stream of authentic reviews without staff involvement.

Problem it solves

Happy patients rarely leave reviews on their own. Clinics with fewer or lower-quality reviews lose patients to competitors with stronger Google reputations — even when the clinical quality is superior. Automated review campaigns solve the ask-at-scale problem that clinics can't do manually.

What it includes
  • Post-appointment WhatsApp review request — Sent automatically at the right time (e.g., a few hours after the appointment), linking directly to the Google review form.
  • Retry sequence — If no review is left within 48h, a polite follow-up message is sent. A second follow-up after 72h if still no action.
  • Review response automation — All new Google reviews (positive and negative) are responded to professionally using pre-approved response templates.
  • Multi-platform support — Requests can be directed to Google, Trustpilot, Doctoralia, or any other relevant platform.
  • Monthly review report — New reviews received, average star rating, review response rate, and ranking impact included in the monthly analytics report.

How it works

1
Appointment completed. The CRM marks the appointment as attended, which triggers the review request sequence.
2
WhatsApp message sent. The patient receives a warm, personalised message thanking them for their visit and inviting them to share their experience on Google.
3
Direct link provided. The message includes a one-tap link to the clinic's Google review page — no searching required.
4
Retry if no action. If no review appears within 48h, a gentle follow-up message is sent automatically.
5
Review responded to. All new reviews are acknowledged promptly, showing prospective patients that the clinic is attentive and professional.

Referral Campaigns

Turn satisfied patients into the clinic's most effective — and most trusted — marketing channel.

What it is

A structured programme that incentivises existing patients to refer friends, family, and colleagues to the clinic. Referrals convert at the highest rate of any acquisition channel because they come with built-in trust — and they cost nothing in ad spend.

Problem it solves

Most clinics rely on referrals happening spontaneously. Without a systematic programme, satisfied patients simply don't think to refer unless prompted. A structured referral campaign makes it easy and incentivises the behaviour — turning goodwill into a reliable acquisition channel.

What it includes
  • Referral programme design — Define the offer structure: what the referring patient receives (discount, free session, gift voucher) and what the new patient receives (welcome discount).
  • Automated referral request messages — WhatsApp and email messages sent to active patients at set intervals, explaining the programme and providing a personalised referral message they can share.
  • Shareable referral links or codes — Easy-to-share format so patients can send to contacts via WhatsApp or messaging apps with minimal effort.
  • Referral tracking — All referred leads are tagged in the CRM so the referring patient is identified and rewarded when the referral converts.

Tracking & Reporting

Know exactly which efforts are generating patients, what they cost, and where to invest next.

What it is

A unified tracking and reporting system that connects every part of the Navigy stack — ads, landing pages, CRM, automation, and organic channels — into clear, actionable monthly reports. We don't just show numbers; we show what they mean and what to do about them.

Problem it solves

Most clinics have no idea which marketing activity generates patients, what a patient costs to acquire, or which channel delivers the best ROI. Without data, spend is guesswork and improvement is impossible. Tracking closes that gap and makes the entire system accountable.

What it includes
  • Full funnel tracking — Every lead's journey is tracked from the ad or organic source, through the landing page, into the CRM, and through to a booked and attended appointment.
  • Google Analytics 4 setup — Conversion events configured for all key actions: form submissions, calls, booking completions, page engagements.
  • Google Ads conversion tracking — Every lead and booking is attributed to the specific campaign, ad group, and keyword that generated it.
  • Meta Pixel + Conversion API — Accurate event tracking across the Meta ecosystem, even with browser cookie restrictions.
  • CRM pipeline reporting — Lead volume, stage-by-stage conversion rates, average time-to-close, and source attribution from the pipeline.
  • CAC & ROI calculation — Cost per lead and cost per acquired patient calculated per channel, per campaign.
  • Monthly performance report — A clear, concise report delivered each month covering: leads generated, appointments booked, channel performance, top-performing ads, and recommended actions for the next month.
  • Strategic review calls — Monthly call to walk through the report, answer questions, and align on priorities for the next period.

How it works

1
Tracking infrastructure installed. GA4, Google Ads, Meta Pixel, and CRM source tags are configured during onboarding. Every lead source is labelled from day one.
2
Live CRM dashboard. The clinic has real-time access to the pipeline — leads, stages, and conversion rates visible at all times through the Navigy platform.
3
Monthly data pull. At month-end we consolidate data from all platforms — ads, website, CRM, WhatsApp, and email — into one clear summary.
4
Analysis & insights. We interpret the data, identifying what worked, what didn't, and the single highest-impact action to take in the next 30 days.
5
Report delivery & review call. The written report is delivered, followed by a strategic call to discuss findings and confirm the next month's priorities.
